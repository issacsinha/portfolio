    • Developing the Frontend of a NGO Website

    Summary

    I had the task of developing the entire frontend of the official website of an NGO about helping dogs, with various functionalities such as donate and adopt options. My responsibilities involved developing a fully functional website from scratch. I also had to make sure that the website was mobile responsive.

    The website was entirely built on HTML5 and CSS3.

    The Webpage

    I developed the entire website of the NGO from scratch, keeping in mind the basic functionalities and features that were needed to showcase in the website. Functionalities such as adoption form and donate option were included, along with showcasing the impact that the NGO had on the lives of abandoned animals. I developed the entire website that included as many as 5 pages.

    Mobile Responsive

    Another major responsibility was to make the website responsive across mobile devices for a better user experience. The website design was tweaked to adjust the mobile viewport.
